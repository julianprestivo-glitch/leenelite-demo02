Privacy Policy

Leen Elite respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy applies to personal data collected via this website, including the space booking form (when enabled and connected in the future).

1) What information do we collect?

  • Contact details: name, email, phone number.
  • Company details: company name, city, CR (optional), VAT number (optional).
  • Request details: exhibition, participation type, category, space size, and notes.
  • Basic technical data (when browsing): browser/device info, pages visited, and cookie data (if any).

2) How do we use your information?

  • Respond to inquiries and follow up on booking/contact requests.
  • Prepare preliminary quotations / participation information upon request.
  • Improve user experience, website performance, and general analytics.
  • Comply with applicable legal or regulatory requirements when necessary.

3) Legal basis

We process your information based on one or more of the following: your consent (e.g., accepting this privacy policy), fulfilling your request (booking/contact follow‑up), or legal obligations.

4) Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting and email) required to operate the website.
  • Regulatory or legal authorities where required by law.

5) Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. When the purpose ends, we delete or anonymize the data where appropriate.

6) Security

We apply reasonable organizational and technical measures to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse, including access controls and secure storage when forms are connected.

7) Your rights

Depending on applicable laws, you may have rights such as accessing, correcting, updating your data, or withdrawing consent (where applicable). You can request this through the contact channels below.

8) Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance your experience and measure performance. You can manage cookies via your browser settings. Disabling cookies may impact certain features.

9) External links

Our website may link to external platforms (e.g., social media). Leen Elite is not responsible for the privacy practices of those third‑party websites.

10) Policy updates

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

11) Contact

For privacy‑related questions or requests, please reach us via Contact.
