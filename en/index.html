Who We Are

Leen Elite is a Saudi event management and marketing company built on a legacy of excellence since 2009. Born from the strength of our sister companies, Silver Chain in safety solutions and X‑Tech in security and IT, we harness technical expertise, trusted partnerships and a skilled team to deliver world‑class events and exhibitions. Our ambition is limitless, inspired by Vision 2030, and we strive to bring global standards to the heart of the Kingdom.

Why Choose Us

We combine local insight with international standards to deliver unforgettable experiences. Our core strengths make us the trusted partner for Saudi Arabia's most iconic events and projects.

  • Deep experience since 2009 across mega projects like King Khalid Airport, NEOM, Misk Schools and Avenues Towers.
  • Strong network of partners and suppliers built over 16 years.
  • Reliable execution with a commitment to quality and innovation.
  • Integrated solutions blending event management, marketing, technology and safety.
  • Understanding of local and international culture and business environment.
  • Investment in human resources: skilled professionals whose talents and team spirit drive our competitive edge.
  • Extensive database covering diverse sectors, multiple contact methods, business leaders, specialists, companies and individuals.
