Who We Are

At Leen Elite, we believe our ambition as a national company is limitless. Inspired by the vision of our inspiring leader, His Royal Highness Prince Mohammed bin Salman, our strength is like that of the Tuwaiq mountain. Our goal is to achieve global reach, originating from the heart of our nation.

Our journey began in 2009, paving the way for excellence in the field of security and safety. We established our solid foothold through our company, Silver Chain, specializing in the supply and installation of fire suppression and alarm systems. This journey has culminated in the execution of mega-projects that we are proud of, such as King Khalid International Airport, NEOM, Misk Schools, and The Avenues Towers, solidifying our position as a trusted partner in major national projects.
